ABOUT US

Discover our Top of the Class Underground Parking.

At Haraya Grand Hotel, we take pride in offering one of the most spacious and well-designed parking facilities in the region. Our underground parking alone can accommodate up to 250 vehicles, while our expansive outdoor parking area provides space for approximately 400 cars, ensuring convenience for all guests even during peak seasons. Despite being a beachfront property—where constructing underground structures is especially challenging due to soil conditions and environmental factors—our hotel successfully achieved a top-of-the-class underground parking facility built with exceptional engineering precision. It is constructed using some of the highest-quality, most durable materials available, ensuring long-term strength, safety, and stability. This world-class project was designed and executed by renowned engineers and architects who are recognized globally for their excellence and innovation, making our parking facility not only spacious and secure, but also a true benchmark of modern structural design.

Best Location

A perfect location for a world-class beachfront destination like Haraya Grand Hotel would be El Nido, Palawan. Known for its breathtaking limestone cliffs, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and powdery white sand beaches, El Nido offers a stunning natural backdrop that perfectly complements luxury and exclusivity. The area is surrounded by pristine islands and hidden lagoons, creating a peaceful and scenic environment ideal for relaxation and high-end tourism. Despite its natural beauty, El Nido continues to develop in accessibility and infrastructure, making it an excellent choice for a premium hotel that blends modern comfort with nature. Positioned in such a remarkable destination, Haraya Grand Hotel would offer guests not only exceptional facilities and services but also an unforgettable tropical experience in one of the most beautiful places in the Philippines.
